# Tanto Mar

## Tagline
**Restoring Coral Reefs for the Future**

---

## Mission

Tanto Mar is dedicated to the innovative **ecological restoration of degraded coral reefs and the conservation of the well-preserved ones.** Our mission is to **restore reefs for the future, not the past** — integrating cutting-edge science, ecological resilience, community-driven solutions, and traditional knowledge to build coral reefs that can survive and thrive in the changing climate.

We go beyond traditional coral restoration by addressing local stressors, such as wastewater pollution, collaborating with local communities to exchange knowledge on sustainable practices, and developing a scalable, science-driven restoration model that can be replicated across South and Central America.

---

## Why Tanto Mar?

Tanto Mar is more than just a reef restoration initiative; it's a bold, pioneering movement to rebuild entire marine ecosystems while empowering coastal communities. As the first NGO dedicated to a truly integrated, science-driven restoration approach, we go beyond coral transplantation to embrace the full complexity of life. From the tiniest microbes to entire ecosystems, we weave together every layer — microbes, corals, fish, and beyond — into a holistic strategy that fosters resilience and sustainability.

But we don't stop at the reefs. Recognizing that marine ecosystems are deeply interconnected with their surroundings, our work bridges the gap between ocean and land. By linking coral reefs with mangroves, seagrasses, and coastal habitats, we are creating a restoration model that strengthens entire ecosystems, tackling the root causes of degradation to ensure long-term recovery.

At its core, our mission is driven by cutting-edge science — but it is also about people. We collaborate closely with local communities, traditional fishers, and Indigenous groups to ensure that reef restoration benefits both nature and those who rely on it. By addressing pollution, enhancing coral resilience through microbiome-based and whole-ecosystem solutions, and providing sustainable livelihoods, Tanto Mar is building a scalable, globally replicable model.

We are not just restoring reefs — we are restoring the balance between people and the ocean, ensuring that both can thrive for generations to come.

Unlike other reef restoration initiatives, Tanto Mar is a new organization focused on integrating ecological, assisted restoration, management of beneficial microbiomes, and socio-environmental sustainability. It is also the **first NGO focused on using an integrated ecological science-driven restoration approach in South America**, and the only one working on the overall restoration of coral reefs in South Bahia.

## Our Vision

A future where coral reefs thrive, where local communities are empowered as stewards of the ocean, and where reef restoration goes beyond coral planting to build whole ecosystems that are resilient, sustainable, and self-sufficient.

Tanto Mar is not just about restoring reefs — it's about restoring the balance between people and nature, ensuring both can coexist and flourish for generations to come.

---

## Our Ambitions

1. **Revive the Blue Planet** – Restore reefs to their full splendor, where corals thrive, marine life flourishes, and ocean ecosystems regain their strength to sustain biodiversity, humanity, and the planet.
2. **Heal Ecosystems, from Land to Sea** – Bridge the gap between marine and terrestrial ecosystems by restoring corals, mangroves, and seagrasses as interconnected lifelines that fortify nature's resilience.
3. **Empower Ocean Guardians** – Inspire and equip local communities, fishers, and Indigenous groups to co-develop knowledge, tools, and sustainable livelihoods to become stewards of their marine heritage.
4. **Scale a Global Movement** – Establish a world-class innovation hub to train and support restoration leaders, transforming neglected reefs across Latin America into thriving ecosystems that set a new standard for conservation worldwide.
5. **Tackle Local Stressors** – Using microbiome-based innovations to treat wastewater and pollution, ensuring healthier reefs; restore whole ecosystems connecting corals, mangroves, and seagrasses.

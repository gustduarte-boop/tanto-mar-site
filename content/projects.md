# Our Actions & Interventions

## 1 → Coral Reef Rewilding & Assisted Ecological Restoration

- Establish nurseries for corals and key reef species to function as biodiversity banks/biovaults to safeguard genetic diversity in situ and ex-situ, ensuring the preservation of key species beyond marine heatwave susceptibility.
- Deploy resilience-based approaches that integrate microbiome management to improve coral survival.
- Build "reefs of the future" — restoration models adapted to survive modern climate challenges, inspired by "Integrated Multitrophic Aquaculture".
- Launch efforts in priority areas neglected by other initiatives (starting in **Corumbau, Brazil**) as innovative models to be replicated worldwide.

## 2 → Address Local Stressors

- Implement nature-based solutions to treat wastewater and local pollution using microbiome-based technology (e.g., biodigestors, soil bioremediation).
- Reduce anthropogenic pressures that weaken reefs, ensuring long-term sustainability through the protection of interconnected ecosystems such as local mangroves.

## 3 → Community-Led Conservation & Capacity Building

- Open space to hear native local communities and develop, based on their needs, actions to improve the environmental conditions and awareness, empowering them and supporting their traditional knowledge.
- Co-develop social technologies, train and employ local communities in coral restoration and sustainable marine management while learning and empowering their traditional knowledge.
- Develop alternative livelihoods to reduce extractive pressures on reefs.
- Strengthen community leadership and governance for long-term conservation impact.

## 4 → Create a Scalable Model for Latin America and Beyond

- Establish **Corumbau** as a global model site for reef restoration and community-driven conservation, capacity building, and training.
- Build a network of restoration hubs in neglected reefs across South and Central America.
- Expand worldwide.
- Share expertise, technology, and best practices to train other organizations in our ecological restoration framework.
